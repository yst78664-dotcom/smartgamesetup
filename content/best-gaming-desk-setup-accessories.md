## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What are the must-have gaming desk accessories?**
A: Monitor arm (ergonomics + desk space), cable management (aesthetics), and extended desk mat (comfort + mouse performance). These three transform any basic desk into a proper gaming station.

**Q: Do I need a monitor arm if my monitor has a stand?**
A: Technically no, but a monitor arm frees 30-50% of desk space, allows perfect ergonomic positioning, and eliminates neck strain. Once you switch, you'll never go back.

**Q: Are RGB desk accessories worth it?**
A: If you enjoy ambient lighting and want a cohesive aesthetic, yes. If purely function-focused, non-RGB alternatives save money without sacrificing performance.

**Q: How much should I spend on gaming desk accessories?**
A: A solid set of essentials costs $100-150 (cable management, budget monitor arm, desk mat). A premium setup runs $400-600. Start with highest-impact items and expand over time.

**Q: Can I use these with a standing desk?**
A: Yes — every product on this list works with standing desks. The Ergotron LX is especially popular with standing desk users due to its wide range of motion.

## Our Verdict

The best gaming desk accessories solve real problems. A monitor arm fixes your posture. Cable management makes your space calmer. A smart power strip saves energy. Start with the basics, upgrade over time, and build a setup that works as hard as you game.
