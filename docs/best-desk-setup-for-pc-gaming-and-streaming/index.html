    Pro Tips from 6 Weeks of Testing

    Desk Placement: Position perpendicular to windows, not facing them. Natural side lighting is flattering; direct sunlight or backlighting ruins your image.

    Cable Planning: Run power and data cables on opposite sides when possible. This reduces electromagnetic interference that can cause audio buzz.

    Lighting Angle: Position key lights 45° above and to the sides of your face. This creates flattering shadows while eliminating harsh direct illumination.

    Audio Distance: Keep your microphone 6-8 inches from your mouth. Closer creates breathing noise; farther picks up room echo and keyboard sounds.

    Stream Deck Setup: Start with basics (scene switching, mute toggle) before building complex macros. Add functions gradually as you identify repetitive tasks.
