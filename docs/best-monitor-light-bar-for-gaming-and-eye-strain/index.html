    Monitor Light Bar vs Desk Lamp: Which is Better for Gaming?

    Traditional Desk Lamp Problems

    ❌ Takes up desk space (mouse pad interference)
    ❌ Creates shadows on keyboard and desk
    ❌ Screen reflections from side angles
    ❌ Uneven lighting across workspace
    ❌ Adjustments needed when changing screen content

    Monitor Light Bar Advantages

    ✅ Zero desk footprint (mounts on monitor)
    ✅ Asymmetric lighting (illuminates desk, not screen)
    ✅ Perfect angle (always optimal position)
    ✅ Uniform coverage of workspace
    ✅ Set-and-forget operation

    Bottom line: Monitor light bars are specifically engineered for computer use, while desk lamps are general-purpose lighting that often creates new problems in gaming setups.

    Installation Guide: Setting Up Your Monitor Light Bar

    Compatibility Check (5 minutes)

    1. Measure monitor thickness: 0.4"-1.6" for most light bars
    2. Check top clearance: 2-3" above screen needed
    3. Identify bezels: Thick gaming monitor bezels may need spacers
    4. Verify power: USB port available (USB-A or USB-C)

    Installation Steps (10 minutes)

    1. Unbox and Inspect (2 min)

    • Check all components included
    • Test touch controls before mounting
    • Ensure counterweight is secure

    2. Position on Monitor (3 min)

    • Center the light bar on your monitor
    • Adjust counterweight for balance
    • Ensure no wobbling when typing

    3. Connect Power (2 min)

    • Use included USB cable
    • Connect to monitor USB port (preferred) or PC
    • Test all brightness levels

    4. Fine-Tune Positioning (3 min)

    • Adjust tilt to eliminate screen reflections
    • Check edge uniformity across desk
    • Test during both bright and dark content

    Pro Setup Tips

    🔧 For ultrawide monitors: Consider two light bars for even coverage
    🔧 For curved monitors: Ensure mounting clips accommodate curve
    🔧 For multi-monitor: Position light bar on primary gaming screen
    🔧 For streaming: Test lighting on camera before going live

    Common Installation Problems & Solutions

    Light Bar Won't Stay Mounted

    Problem: Falls off monitor during use
    Solution: Clean monitor top with alcohol, check weight distribution, add adhesive pads if needed

    Screen Reflections Still Visible

    Problem: Light reflects off screen surface
    Solution: Adjust tilt angle, ensure asymmetric beam design, check for curved monitor incompatibility

    USB Power Issues

    Problem: Light bar dims or flickers
    Solution: Use monitor USB port instead of PC, check cable connections, avoid USB hubs

    Uneven Desk Lighting

    Problem: Bright spots or shadows on workspace
    Solution: Adjust height and angle, ensure proper distance from desk, check for obstructions
