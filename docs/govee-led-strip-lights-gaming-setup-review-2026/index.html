    Common Issues & Solutions

    WiFi Connection Problems

    Problem: Strip won't connect to WiFi
    Solution: Ensure 2.4GHz network, move router closer during setup, reset strip by holding power for 10 seconds

    Screen Sync Latency

    Problem: Lights lag behind screen (DreamView G1)
    Solution: Close unnecessary apps, update desktop software, adjust sync sensitivity in app

    Adhesive Failure

    Problem: Strip falls off after weeks/months
    Solution: Clean surface with alcohol, use additional 3M mounting tape, avoid high-heat areas

    Color Accuracy Issues

    Problem: Colors look washed out or incorrect
    Solution: Adjust brightness to 80%, calibrate in app, check ambient light sensor settings
