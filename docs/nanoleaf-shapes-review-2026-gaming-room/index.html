    Installation Guide: Mounting Nanoleaf Shapes in Your Gaming Room

    Planning Your Layout (30 minutes)

    Design first, buy later: Use the Nanoleaf app's Layout Assistant to plan your design before purchasing panels. This prevents costly mistakes.

    Wall considerations:

    • ✅ Flat, painted drywall (best adhesion)
    • ✅ At least 6 feet from your gaming chair (optimal viewing)
    • ❌ Avoid textured walls or wallpaper
    • ❌ Don't mount above heat sources (consoles, radiators)

    What You'll Need

    • 📱 Nanoleaf app (design planning)
    • 📐 Level and measuring tape
    • 🧽 Isopropyl alcohol wipes
    • 🔌 Power outlet within 6 feet
    • ⏰ 2-3 hours for first-time installation

    Step-by-Step Installation

    1. Design Planning (30 min)

    • Use Nanoleaf app's Layout Assistant
    • Take photos of your wall space
    • Create 2-3 design options
    • Mark power adapter location

    2. Wall Preparation (30 min)

    • Clean wall with alcohol wipes
    • Mark panel positions with painter's tape
    • Ensure level measurements
    • Test one panel's adhesion first

    3. Panel Mounting (60-90 min)

    • Start from power adapter position
    • Work outward following your design
    • Press firmly for 30 seconds per panel
    • Connect linking connectors carefully

    4. App Setup (15 min)

    • Download Nanoleaf app
    • Create account and add panels
    • Connect to WiFi (2.4GHz required)
    • Install desktop Screen Mirror app

    Pro installation tips:

    • 🎯 Buy 1-2 extra panels for future expansion
    • 🔧 Use additional 3M strips for extra security
    • 🎨 Leave gaps between shapes for dramatic shadows
    • ⚡ Place controller hub centrally for best connectivity
