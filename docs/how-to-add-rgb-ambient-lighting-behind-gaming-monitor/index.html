    Step-by-Step Installation

    Step 1: Clean the Monitor Back

    • Power off and unplug your monitor
    • Use isopropyl alcohol wipe to clean the back edge
    • Let dry for 2 minutes for maximum adhesion

    Step 2: Plan Your Layout

    • Measure your monitor's perimeter
    • Mark where you'll start the strip (usually bottom-left)
    • Leave the power cable connection accessible

    Step 3: Apply the LED Strip

    • Peel 2-3 inches of backing tape at a time
    • Press firmly along the monitor's back edge
    • Pro tip: Keep the strip 1 inch from the edge so light diffuses properly
    • Make clean corners by folding, not cutting

    Step 4: Connect and Test

    • Plug in the power adapter
    • Use the remote to test all colors
    • Adjust brightness to comfortable level (start at 50%)

    Pro Tips for Perfect RGB Monitor Lighting

    ⚡ Positioning Secrets

    Distance from wall matters: Place your monitor 12-18 inches from the wall. Too close and you get harsh light spots. Too far and the effect disappears.

    Height placement: LED strip should be 1 inch inside the monitor edge, not on the very rim. This creates smooth light diffusion instead of harsh lines.

    🛠️ Troubleshooting Common Issues

    Strips won't stick:

    • Clean with alcohol, wait to dry
    • Use extra 3M mounting tape if needed
    • Avoid dusty or textured surfaces

    Colors look washed out:

    • Increase LED brightness to 70%+
    • Check for ambient room light interference
    • Some cheap strips have weak color output

    Screen sync lag:

    • Update Govee app to latest version
    • Close other RGB software (conflicts happen)
    • Recalibrate camera if colors drift over time
